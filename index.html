Features

About

This work is supported by the European Commission's Seventh Framework Programme.
Activity ICT (FP7-ICT-2013.8.1), Grant agreement no: 611383.

  • 2015

    Initial idea

    Build a system that takes advantage of NLP tasks and machine-learning techniques to provide useful information from a collection of documents.

  • January 2016

    Transition to services

    Then librairy starts to take shape. An event-oriented system based on microservices is being cooked maintaining an overall balance between scalability principles, efficiency and simplicity.
