Now we tell you which are the skills that we are looking for:

Backend Developer


We are seeking a highly skilled Artificial Intelligence Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate should have a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence, with demonstrable experience in natural language processing and machine learning. Additionally, they should have advanced programming skills and experience deploying AI solutions in virtual environments.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop natural language processing and machine learning models to address specific business challenges.
  • Design and implement machine learning algorithms that are efficient and scalable.
  • Conduct data analysis and generate reports on the results obtained.
  • Code and implement AI solutions in virtual environments.
  • Participate in technical decision-making for the AI team.
  • Collaborate with other teams in the company to integrate AI solutions into the existing infrastructure.

Requirements:

  • Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence or related fields.
  • Demonstrable experience in natural language processing and machine learning.
  • Advanced programming skills in languages such as Python, R, and Java.
  • Experience in developing AI solutions in virtual environments.
  • Knowledge of deep learning frameworks such as Tensorflow, PyTorch, or Keras.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work in a team.
  • Ability to quickly learn new technologies and processes.

We Offer:

  • Competitive salary based on the candidate's experience and skills (30-50k€).
  • Opportunity to work in a growing company with a dynamic and passionate team.
  • Career growth plan and personalized development opportunities.
  • Creative and innovative work environment.

Frontend Developer


We are looking for a highly skilled Web Designer specialized in document processing, with advanced programming skills and experience in deploying web design solutions in virtual environments. The ideal candidate must have experience in designing and developing innovative websites and a deep understanding of natural language and text processing.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop websites that reflect the company's brand image, with a focus on optimizing user experience and usability.
  • Deploy document processing and machine learning techniques to improve website functionality and efficiency.
  • Work with other team members to ensure effective integration of document processing and machine learning techniques into the overall architecture of the websites.
  • Maintain and update existing websites to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Work with clients and other teams in the company to understand and meet their web design needs.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Web Design, Computer Science, or related fields.
  • Proven experience in designing and developing innovative websites.
  • Advanced programming skills in languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, among others.
  • Knowledge of design tools such as Sketch, Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, among others.
  • Experience in deploying web design solutions in virtual environments.
  • Ability to work in a team and collaborate with other departments.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work in a dynamic work environment.

We Offer:

  • Competitive salary commensurate with the candidate's experience and skills.
  • Opportunity to work in a growing company with a dynamic and passionate team.
  • Personalized professional growth plan and development opportunities.
  • Creative and innovative work environment.
  • Flexible schedule and relaxed work environment.

If you meet the above requirements and you like the idea of working with us, please send your updated resume and a cover letter summarizing your relevant experience and motivation for applying. We look forward to hearing from you!
